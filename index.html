Automatic Sprinkler

Goal

Automatically waters a plant (currently in service since 08 July 2022, photo taken on 28 November 2022)

How it works

An arduino captures the humidity in the ground and activates or not the pump so that the humidity is maintained between two levels. By using the water contained in any tank set aside.

Automatic Sprinkler V2

Goal

Automatically waters the plant in the upper part of the pot.

Functioning

An electronic circuit of my design manages the watering so that the humidity of the soil in the pot is maintained.
The pot is sealed via a seal which allows water to be retained in the two lower parts and a hole at the back allows the tank to be filled.

Components

- 3 parts modeled and printed in 3D
- A pump
- Electronic components (transistors, resistances, LED, LM324, NE555)

Vinted Bot

Goal

Robot looking for an item at the best price on Vinted.

Functioning

By sending a command to the robot on a Discord chat, we launch a search for an article. We can choose a size, a brand, a maximum price, the condition of the object sought, its brand...
Searches are kept in a database so that the robot can search continuously until an article comes out to be the first notified of its exit.
We can choose to receive a Discord message when the robot finds an article with the image, and the link of the article.
